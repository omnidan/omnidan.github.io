Supporting me

First of all, you can fork my repositories on github and work on them. It makes me very happy when my projects are being worked on, but I don't have enough time to do so on my own on most projects.

Secondly, you can check out and maybe use my products and software, this also makes me happy. It shows me that what I'm doing isn't totally useless.

Thirdly, you can give me some feedback. Whether positive or negative, I always enjoy reading good feedback, that means, if you give me a feedback, please elaborate on the subject and explain me why I did something good/bad and how I could do better.

Lastly, you can donate money to me. With this money I can fund new and existing projects.
